Real world health systems are complex and adaptive, demanding a complete system-wide awareness of effective interventions, and design methodologies appropriate to the complexity of the contexts.

Impact

Working across global public health sectors with a focus on child survival and logistics, Field is driven by a critical examination of impact and an evidence based approach to solution design.
